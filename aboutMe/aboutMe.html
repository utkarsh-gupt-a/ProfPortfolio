About Me
  • Dr. Rajesh Kumar is a Professor of Civil Engineering at IIT (BHU) Varanasi, specializing in structural optimization, non-linear mechanics, finite element analysis, earthquake engineering, and concrete technology.

  • He holds a Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur and brings over 24 years of combined teaching, research, and consultancy experience.

  • Dr. Kumar has published extensively, guided numerous postgraduate and doctoral students, and received national recognition for his work on soil-structure interaction and sustainable construction materials.

  • He has also earned prestigious awards, holds patents for engineering innovations, contributed to major consultancy projects, and served in key administrative roles at IIT (BHU).
